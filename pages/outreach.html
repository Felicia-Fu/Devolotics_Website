    About Us

    Robotics isn't all about the robots. This year we've dedicated a great deal of time and effort into educating our community about STEM. We created the Scarborough Stem Alliance (SSA), a non-profit organization aimed at inspiring youth with STEM, and through this organization we've hosted numerous robotics and programming workshops at different local schools and our local public library. We believe that the next generation relies on STEM, so we hope to empower today's students to learn all about it.
